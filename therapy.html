Therapy Approach

My sessions are available in English and Russian language. Online sessions are available via Zoom or Skype, making it convenient to attend remotely from anywhere in the world. In therapy, you will get the reflective space and time that is exclusively yours.

After the first session, where we would have the opportunity to meet each other, explore your story and issues you might be experiencing, I will offer you open-ended work on a weekly basis or more frequent if you find it useful.

We will work together towards identifying some unapparent and recurring patterns that underpin your behaviours, interpersonal relationships and life-long emotional experiences. Whatever personal or interpersonal issues you are facing, first of all I work with you as an individual with your own unique personal history.

All sessions last 50 minutes.
